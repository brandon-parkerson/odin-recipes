Spaghetti

Description:

An Italian dish enjoyable to everyone!

Ingredients:

  • Spaghetti pasta
  • meat
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Tomato sauce
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic
  • Basil
  1. Boil salted water
  2. Once boiling, add the pasta
  3. Start cooking the meat
  4. Chop garlic and basil
  5. Add chopped Ingredients to the meat
  6. Drain the finished pasta
  7. Add tomato sauce to the meat
  8. Place pasta in dish and add meat on top of the pasta
  9. Enjoy!
