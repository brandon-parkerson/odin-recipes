Blueeberry Pancakes

Description

An American classic to satisfy the soul

Ingredients:

  • Pancake batter
  • Blueberries
  • Syrup
  • Butter

Steps:

  1. Start making the pancake batter. If you don't know how to do this... google it.
  2. Make sure the batter is nice and smooth
  3. Heat up a pan on low to medium heat
  4. Add butter to the pan
  5. Pour batter onto the pan
  6. Add the blueberries to the liquid batter on the pan
  7. Cook for about 20 seconds and then flip
  8. Repeat for number of pancakes you want
  9. Place on a plate and add syrup and butter on top
  10. Enjoy!
