Banana Split

Description

Delicious Ice-cream dish

Ingredients:

  • Ripe bananas
  • Vanilla Ice-cream
  • Chocolate Ice-cream
  • Whipped cream
  • Hot fudge
  • Chopped nuts
  • Sprinkles

Steps:

  1. Place banana in a long dish
  2. Add scoops of both flavors of Ice-creams
  3. Add the whipped cream on top
  4. Add your chopped nuts
  5. Then finally top off with sprinkles
  6. Enjoy!
